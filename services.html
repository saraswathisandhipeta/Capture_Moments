Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a photography session? +

You can book directly through our website by selecting your preferred service and photographer, or contact us for a custom quote. We require a 25% deposit to secure your booking.

What's included in the service price? +

All our packages include the photographer's time, professional editing of images, and digital delivery via an online gallery. Additional costs may apply for prints, albums, or special requests.

How long until I receive my photos? +

Standard delivery time is 2-3 weeks for most sessions. Wedding photography may take 4-6 weeks due to the volume of images. Rush processing is available for an additional fee.

Can I request a specific photographer? +

Absolutely! When booking, you can browse our photographer profiles and select your preferred professional based on their portfolio and specialty.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We understand plans change. Cancellations made more than 14 days before the session receive a full refund of the deposit. Within 14 days, the deposit is non-refundable but can be applied to a future session within 6 months.
